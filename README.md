Data on weather in Brzoskwinia are web scraped on a daily basis from airly web page. They are added to the dataset, cleaned and extracted. PM1, PM2.5 and PM10 concentrations are analysed in dependence on weather factors: humidity, temperature and pressure. Average concentrations of pollutants are calculated throughout hours of the day and plotted online using Plotly Chart Studio. Correlation between factors is checked as well as humidity data spread. Daily exceedings of PM10 concentrations are checked.
